Puri


Puri is a charming coastal city in Odisha, India, offering a perfect blend of spirituality and natural beauty. With its iconic Jagannath Temple and the annual Rath Yatra festival, it attracts devotees and tourists alike. The golden beaches of Puri provide a serene environment for relaxation and water sports. A visit to the famous Sun Temple in Konark is a must to witness its architectural grandeur. The region's delectable seafood cuisine and vibrant local markets add to the charm of this delightful holiday destination.
